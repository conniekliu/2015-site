Duet

The Problem

How can we design clothing that enables people with muscular dystrophy with more freedom?

The Solution

A pair of pants with detachable underwear that is easy to don and doff.

Duet is a comfortable pair designed with expandable cloth panels zipped into the sides and attached underwear to make it easy to don and doff. From getting dressed in the morning to using public restrooms on the go, Duet makes your life easier no matter where you are.
